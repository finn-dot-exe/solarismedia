WHAT IS SOLARIS MEDIA?

Solaris Media is a gaming and tech multimedia blog that focuses on providing reviews and thoughts on video games, gaming culture, tech, and the internet from a unique perspective - None of that seemingly endless stream of copy and pasted buzzwords you often see on other sites. We aim to provide a more personal, engaging, and sometimes humorous take on the topics we cover. The goal is to have a place to decompress and share thoughts on the things we love, without the woes of being overly professional or polished in writing.

OUR TEAM

finn.exe - Owner, Editor-In-Chief

Finn is the founder and editor-in-chief of Solaris Media. Tech tinkerer and gamer from early childhood, Finn has a passion for all things gaming, but especially Kojima-san. Finn is a huge fan of story-based single-player experiences, and has a particular love for Atlus, Square and Kojima Production titles. Outside of gaming, Finn is an ameteur Muay Thai fighter, and has even acted in a Shakespeare production.

OUR STORY

Solaris Media originally started as a record label focused on the DIY/DIT music scene, but has since evolved into a multimedia blog after no longer wanting to be responsible for running a label. The transition is a natural one, as the label dabbled in bootleg video game OST's in its later years, and Finn also streamed on Twitch and YouTube for many years. This gives us a place to continue to share the things we love at our own pace.
